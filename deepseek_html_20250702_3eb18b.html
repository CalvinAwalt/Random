Complex Adaptive Systems in AI

Polycentric Systems

Multiple independent AI agents make mutual adjustments within a shared rule framework. This enables decentralized problem-solving and robust adaptation to changing environments.

Hierarchic Systems

AI systems organized in nested hierarchies, with higher-level systems coordinating specialized subsystems. This enables complex problem-solving through abstraction and specialization.

Cybernetic Feedback

Information feedback loops allow AI systems to self-regulate and adapt. Positive feedback reinforces successful patterns while negative feedback corrects errors.

Self-Improvement & Destruction

AI systems continuously evolve through self-modification. Components that prove valuable are strengthened while ineffective ones are pruned, enabling constant adaptation.
